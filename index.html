2 Hour Design Workshop

The 2-Hour Design Workshop on Figma is a Weekend based workshop that helps you learn UI/UX Design. In the workshop, we choose a tech product and design 2-3 modules of it along with teaching basics of UI/UX Design and Figma.

The workshop is divided down into six parts. First, participants are introduced to design principles. Then, we go to the Fundamentals of Figma. We learn to use Figma and its best practices.

The next step is to start designing the Tech product. We pick 1 module and design it in Figma. The last step is an assignment that we give you to practice your understanding of the workshop.

Overall, this workshop is designed to uplift your way in UI/UX Design learning. The workshop is suitable for beginners who are new to Figma or for those who have some experience but want to improve their skills.
